## Calculting mean in CSV
For getting the mean, the annual daily rainfall files were summed up spatially, and a mean was taken over the shapefile defined area. 
Since the rainfall is 0.25 x 0.25 degree, it was coarse resolution for city analysis, a buffer of 0.25 degree was considered while calculating the mean rainfall as multiple grids were falling in the shapefile. 
To maintain consistency the builtup was also calculated with 0.25 degree buffer.
